In this state, There is enough wind power from the Turbines to power the whole of the Electrical Grid. As such, the Power Stations are turned off.

Due to the fact that there is a surplus of energy being created, the grid diverts the excess energy into battery storage, for use in shortages.

There is some energy being injected into the grid from an offshore wave generator, on the floor of the ocean.

There is enough Sunlight for the Factory and the Farm to generate their own energy.

There is enough energy to charge the electric cars parked at the charging stations

There is enough energy to power both the shops and the homes that are connected to the grid.
